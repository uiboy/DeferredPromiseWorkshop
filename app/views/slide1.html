Deferred and Promises in AngularJS

  • Deferred:

  • -Is a state storage object. It is used to represent an asynchronous operation. Which can be successful or not.

  • Promise:

  • -Represens the result of an asynchronous operation. May or may not be finished at any given point in time

  • -Attribute of deferred object

  • -Chainable

  • -Usable in $q.all
